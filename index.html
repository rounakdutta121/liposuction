Who May Be A Good Candidate?

You may be a suitable candidate for liposuction if you have stubborn fat in specific areas, stable weight, good health, realistic expectations, and are ready to follow recovery instructions.

Stable Weight

Your weight should be relatively stable for at least 6 months before considering liposuction.

Localized Fat Deposits

Fat in specific areas like the abdomen, thighs, arms, or chin that does not respond to diet and exercise.

Good Overall Health

No major medical conditions that could impair healing or increase surgical risk.

Realistic Expectations

Understanding that liposuction is a body-contouring procedure, not a weight-loss solution.

Commitment to Recovery

Willingness to follow post-operative instructions, wear compression garments, and attend follow-up visits.

What Happens During Liposuction?

Liposuction is a surgical body-contouring procedure performed using a thin tube called a cannula to remove localized fat deposits.

1

Consultation & Planning

Dr. Akhil Garg evaluates your body type, fat distribution, skin quality, and health condition. A personalized surgical plan is created based on your aesthetic goals.

2

Anesthesia & Incision

The procedure is performed under local or general anesthesia. Small incisions are made in inconspicuous areas to access the targeted fat deposits.

3

Fat Removal & Contouring

A thin cannula is inserted through the incisions to gently suction out localized fat deposits. The surgeon sculpts the area for a smoother, more proportionate contour.

Recovery After Liposuction

Swelling, bruising or mild discomfort may occur. A compression garment may be advised. Most patients return to light activities in a few days.

First 24-48 Hours

Rest recommended. Mild discomfort, swelling, and bruising are normal. Compression garment applied.

First Week

Light activities resume. Swelling subsides. Compression garment worn as advised.

2-4 Weeks

Return to normal routine. Gradual contour improvement. Light exercise after clearance.

1-6 Months

Final results apparent. Scarring fades. Enjoy improved body contour.

Recovery Tips

Wear compression garment as directed
Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet
Avoid strenuous activity for 2-4 weeks
Attend all scheduled follow-up appointments
Keep incisions clean and dry
Follow medication schedule as prescribed
