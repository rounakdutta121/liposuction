Doctor-led liposuction consultation

Every Body is Different. Every Plan is Personal.

Liposuction is not a weight-loss treatment. It is a surgical body-contouring procedure used for localized fat pockets that do not respond well to regular diet and exercise.

During your consultation, Dr. Akhil Garg will evaluate your body type, fat distribution, skin quality, health condition and aesthetic goals before advising whether liposuction is suitable for you.

Suitability matters

Who May Be a Good Candidate?

You may be a suitable candidate for liposuction if you have stubborn fat in specific areas, stable weight, good health, realistic expectations and are ready to follow recovery instructions.

Stable Weight

Weight should be relatively stable before considering liposuction.

Localized Fat Deposits

Specific areas that do not respond well to diet and exercise.

Good Overall Health

No major condition that may increase surgical or healing risk.

Recovery Commitment

Follow instructions, wear compression garment and attend follow-ups.

Procedure overview

What Happens During Liposuction?

Liposuction is a surgical body-contouring procedure performed using a thin tube called a cannula to remove localized fat deposits.

1

3

Fat Removal & Contouring

Localized fat deposits are gently suctioned to create a smoother, more proportionate contour.

Recovery guidance

Recovery After Liposuction

Swelling, bruising or mild discomfort may occur. A compression garment may be advised. Many patients return to light activities in a few days, depending on the procedure and medical advice.

First 24-48 Hours

Rest, prescribed medication and compression as advised.

First Week

Light activity may resume after surgeon guidance.

2-4 Weeks

Gradual return to routine depending on healing.

Weeks to Months

Contour changes become clearer as swelling settles.
